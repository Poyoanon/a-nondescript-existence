# Lost Ark Nexus
[Lost Ark Nexus](https://www.lostark.nexus) is an info aggregator website containing guides, information and infographics for a highly popular Korean MMO, *Lost Ark* from Smilegate RPG for the Western version of the game. The guides on this website are made by several members of the community who actively love and play this game, of which I am also a contributor to.

Made with
* React
* NextJS
* Tailwind CSS
* Zustand
